Klaye's Life:

The Beginning

Hola amigos! I am Princess Kaye Geonzon, a 19-year old woman from Minglanilla, Cebu, Philippines. Currently, I am in my second year in college, pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Cebu Technological University - Main Campus. Get to know me by exploring this page!

ABOUT ME

I am Princess Kaye, an “aspirant” in life.

Children would always have silly dreams to share. I vividly remember how I would always tell others that I would become a doctor. However, as most people are, their childhood dreams can be different from their lifetime dreams. Dreams could change. I saw how my friends slowly found out what they wanted to do in life. They got to know themselves better, and they discovered what they were passionate about. Honestly, I was envious. I grew up, and I realized I didn’t want to become a doctor. That’s fine, dreams change. However, I didn’t have a specific dream to replace the dream I used to have. I felt hopeless. Others are honing their skills to achieve their goals, but I am still struggling to know where I belong. I was anxious, and I felt like I was left behind. I forced myself to like something. I explored numerous fields only to fail. When I was in my senior year in high school, I was conflicted. I have to decide for my career, and this involves my future. The decision I’ll make will decide what type of life I will have. I did my research, and I realized something. I find Science interesting. I can’t say I am passionate about it, but it did pique my interest. As such, I decided to take up Information Technology. It was on a whim. Now that I am here, I don’t regret it, but I can’t say I’m passionate about it. I believe I will soon find my place and purpose. I am an aspirant who yearns to discover the world to know who I am. I created the conclusion that I don’t have to be as passionate as others, and I can only live once, so I shouldn’t dwell on pursuing what others have that I don’t, right? I want to live happily today, so I am doing that. I aspire to live and smile.
